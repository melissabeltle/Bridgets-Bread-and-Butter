About Us

We offer an experience that changes perceptions of people with disabilities, through a person-first business model. The integration of food, art, and inclusive employment is our recipe for spreading change. As a social enterprise, we maximize our impact on community livelihoods by investing in each other.

Mission

We change perceptions of people with disabilities by integrating food, art and inclusive employment.
