Welcome! At Bridget's Bread & Butter, We change perceptions of people with disabilities by integrating food, art, and inclusive employment.

We offer an experience that transforms perceptions of people with disabilities, through a person-first business model. The integration of food, art, and inclusive employment is our recipe for spreading change. As a social enterprise, we maximize our impact on communities by investing in each other.
